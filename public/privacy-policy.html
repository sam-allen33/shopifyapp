What this app does

This app requests shipping rates during checkout. It uses data required to return shipping rates and to keep the app running reliably.

Data we process

  • Store and session information from Shopify needed for authentication and app operation.
  • Merchant-provided Shipwise bearer token used to request rates from Shipwise.
  • Shipping request information needed to calculate rates (for example destination and cart shipping context).
  • Product shipping data (for example variant weight and shipping dimensions) used to improve rate accuracy.
  • Limited technical and debugging data, such as correlation IDs, statuses, counts, and latency.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell merchant or customer data.
  • We do not use data from this app for advertising.
  • We do not collect credit card information through this app.

Retention

  • Normal application logs target: 7 days, where infrastructure settings allow.
  • Session records: up to 30 days.
  • Shipwise bearer token: stored while needed for app functionality; deleted promptly after uninstall or when no longer needed.

Some retention settings are controlled by hosting providers (for example Render logging settings) and may require manual configuration outside application code.

Data deletion requests and Shopify privacy webhooks

This app implements Shopify privacy webhooks for customers/data_request, customers/redact, and shop/redact. For shop-level deletion and uninstall events, stored shop-level app data is deleted.
